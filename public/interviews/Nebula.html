NEBULA by Martux_m

 

"One does not exist in the world, one becomes with the world, everything is vision, one becomes universe, one becomes animal, vegetable, molecular, one becomes zero,"

speed of movement:

letting oneself be carried by the flow, rhythms and melodies intertwining and evolving, creating a kind of sound landscape in constant transformation of movement and change. the work invites a sensory journey through soundscapes that transform and evolve like fog moved by the wind.

without feeling obligated to analyze or interpret every single note.
